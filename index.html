Who Are We

Tanashe food concept is a food science and technology research and development company, providing in-depth scientific consultation, skills, knowledge, and training. Tanashe's areas of expertise are focused on novel food product development, design of colloidal/emulsion-based delivery systems and functional foods, encapsulation of bioactives, utilisation of neglected plant protein sources, food structure and stability, and valorisation of food processing by-products.
