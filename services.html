What we Do

We drive food innovation by providing and transferring excellent scientific knowledge and support to the food industry. We promote strong collaborations and partnerships enabling the translation of novel ideas to real food applications that positively impact the health and well-being of west African consumers.

Food colloids/emulsion
Food colloids/emulsionclose

Our food colloids research focuses on the formation, interfacial structural characteristics, chemical stability, and practical food applications of colloidal delivery systems such as emulsions, foams and gels.

Food Stability
Food Stabilityclose

Our food stability group provides expertise on physical and chemical stability of foods to ensure accurate product stability claims. The influence of various processing parameters or conditions on final product stability provides good insight into instability challenges.

Food Bioactives
Food Bioactivesclose

Our food bioactive platform explores naturally available compounds in foods or food by-products which can confer health benefits such anti-inflammatory, anti-cancer etc. on food consumers, and focuses on their stability and plausible utilisation in development of functional foods.

New Product Development
New Product Developmentclose

Our new product development stream brings together excellent science and engineering, and culinary skills to translate ‘out of the world’ new food product ideas to real food products and provides support in moving prototype products to commercialisation.

Food quality and regulatory affairs
Food quality and regulatory affairs close

Our food quality and regulatory affairs stream provides support on improving the textural and sensory properties of foods to enhance positive consumer perception and acceptability of healthy foods, and expertise on food laws about food labelling, nutrition and health claims, and novel foods.

Neglected nutrient sources
Neglected nutrient sourcesclose

This platform seeks to increase the utilisation of neglected and highly underutilised nutrient (proteins, vitamins, lipids etc) sources by exploring a deeper understanding of their functional properties, stability, and plausible food applications as well as the potential synergetic functionality that could be derived through blending with conventional foods.

Research training
Research trainingclose

We provide training and tips to students and researchers on effective ways to search for research articles, using referencing software and general manuscript preparations. This platform also provides support on research design and analysis.
